About VoteDigital



VoteDigital is a web-based voting system that will help you manage your voting based selections easily and securely. This voting system can be used for casting votes during the elections held in colleges, etc. In this system the voter do not have to go to the polling booth to cast their vote. They can use their personal computer to cast their votes. All the names of the voters with their complete information is stored. Voters can simply register by filling a registration form. If invalid/wrong details are submitted, then the person is not registered to vote. After registration, the voter should log in to their respective profile through thr login form. After logging in to the profile, all the participants, that are taking part in the elections, are visible. Now, the voter is all set to vote for the respective favorite participant. And yes, it's done..!!! And there is no question of voting second time as the voter loses the access to vote right after voting once. The advantage of online voting is that the voters have the choice of voting at their own free time and there is reduced congestion. It also minimizes on errors of vote counting.
